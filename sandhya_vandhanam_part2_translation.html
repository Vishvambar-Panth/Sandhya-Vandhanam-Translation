Verse 1 — Invocation of Gaṇeśa (child delights Śiva & Pārvatī)
वात्सल्यात्पतरौ कपोलयुगलं स्वस्यागतौ चुम्बितुं दृष्ट्वाऽऽडकुञ्चित मास्यपद्ममलं सेषिस्मतं सत्वरम् । अन्योन्य शिवयोस्तत: सुवदनेयुक्ते अभूतां तयो: इत्थं येनविनोदितौ स भगवान्बालो गजास्योऽवतु ॥ १ ॥
vātsalyāt patarau kapolayugalaṃ svasyāgatau cumbituṃ / dṛṣṭvā ḍakuñcita māsyapadmamalaṃ seṣismataṃ satvaram / anyonya śivayostataḥ suvadaneyukte abhūtāṃ tayoḥ / itthaṃ yenavino ditau sa bhagavānbālo gajāsyo'vatu

Verse 6 — Never abandon dharma; it is eternal
न जातुकामान्भयान्लोभाद्धर्मत्यजेज्जीवितस्यापि हेतो: । धर्मोनित्य: सुखदु:खेत्वनित्ये जीवोनित्यो हेतुरस्यत्वनित्य: ॥ ६ ॥

"OM. Fire is ash · Wind is ash · Water is ash · Earth is ash · Sky/Ether is ash · All this is truly ash · Even the mind and these eyes are ash." Having consecrated with mantras, with ash mixed with water held by the middle three fingers, one should apply the tripuṇḍra (three horizontal ash lines) at the forehead, heart, navel, throat, shoulders, arm-joints, back, and head — using the mantra "OM Namaḥ Śivāya," or the Gāyatrī, or the Praṇava (OM).

॥ यज्ञोपवीतधारणविधिः ॥ — Procedure declaration
आचम्य, प्राणानायम्य, देशकालौ संकीर्त्य । श्रौतस्मार्त विहित नित्यकर्मानुष्ठान योगयता सिद्धचर्थें, यज्ञोपवीतधारणं करिष्ये । यज्ञोपवीतं, दशवारं, गायत्र्या, अभिमन्त्र्य । यज्ञोपवीत मित्यस्य परब्रह्म परमात्मा त्रिष्टुप्, यज्ञोपवीतधारणे विनियोग: ।
After performing Ācamana (water sipping), Prāṇāyāma (breath-regulation), and declaring the place and time — in order to attain qualification for performing the daily rituals enjoined by Śruti and Smṛti — I shall wear the sacred thread. Having consecrated the sacred thread ten times with the Gāyatrī mantra. For this mantra "Yajñopavīta," whose deity is the Supreme Brahman (Paramātmā), in the meter Triṣṭup — the application is in wearing the sacred thread.

oṃ yajñopavītaṃ paramaṃ pavitraṃ prajāpater yatsahajaṃ purastāt / āyuṣyamagram prati muñca śubhraṃ yajñopavītaṃ balamastu tejaḥ
"OM. The sacred thread is supremely holy, born together with Prajāpati (the Creator) in the beginning. Wear this bright white thread that promotes long life and excellence — may the sacred thread be strength and radiance."

Instruction — wear thread on right arm first, then around neck; dispose old thread
इति मंत्रेण प्रथमं दक्षिण बाहुमुद्धृत्य पश्चात्कंठे धारयेत् । आचम्य । उपवीतं मिञ्जतन्तुं जीर्णं कश्मलदूषितम् । विसृजामि जले ब्रह्मन्वर्चो दीर्घायुरस्तुमे ॥ इति, जीर्णयज्ञोपवीतं, 'समुद्रं गच्छस्वाहे'ति जले विसर्जयेत् ।
By this mantra, first holding it on the right arm, then wear it around the neck. Then perform Ācamana. "I immerse in water this old, frayed, impurity-tainted sacred thread. O Brahman, may radiance and long life be mine." — Saying thus, the old sacred thread should be immersed in water with the words "Go to the sea — svāhā."
