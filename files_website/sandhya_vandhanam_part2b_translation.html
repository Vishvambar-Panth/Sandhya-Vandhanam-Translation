Prāṇāyāma — For the Praṇava (OM): the sage is Para-Brahman, the deity is the Supreme Self (Paramātmā), the meter is the divine Gāyatrī; its purpose is in Prāṇāyāma. [Recite the seven vyāhṛtis:] OM Bhūḥ · OM Bhuvaḥ · OM Svaḥ · OM Mahaḥ · OM Janaḥ · OM Tapaḥ · OM Satyam. [Then:] "OM — We meditate upon the supreme radiance of the divine Savitā; may He inspire our intellects." "OM — Water, Light, Essence, Immortality, Brahman — OM Bhūr Bhuvaḥ Svaḥ OM."

Aghamarṣaṇa (Expulsion of the Sin-Person) — For this three-verse hymn beginning "Ṛtaṃ ca": sage Aghamarṣaṇa, deity Bhāvavṛtta, meter Anuṣṭubh; its purpose is in expelling the sin-person. "OM. From the great Tapas (ardour) were born Truth and Cosmic Order (Ṛta). From that arose night. From that arose the ocean. From the ocean and from the ocean arose the year. Day and night rule over all of creation. The Creator set the Sun and Moon in place as before — heaven, earth, and the atmosphere, and the higher worlds." — Holding the breath through the left nostril, expel the sin-person through the right nostril, cast that water to the left side without looking at it. Then perform Ācamana again.

भूर्भुव: स्व: तत्सवितुर्वरेण्यं भर्गो देवस्य धीमहि । धियोयोन: प्रचोदयात् ॥ श्री सूर्यसावित्र्यै नम इदमर्घ्यं समर्पयामि, एवं त्रि: । काल्तिक्रमदोष परिहारार्थं प्रायश्चित्ताधर्घप्रदानं करिष्ये, इदमपि गायत्र्यैव ॥ असावादित्यो ब्रह्म, इत्यात्मानं प्रदक्षिणं परिफिच्य अप उपस्पृशेत् ॥ तत: पुनराचम्य ।
"OM Bhūr Bhuvaḥ Svaḥ — We meditate on the supreme radiance of the divine Savitā; may He inspire our intellects." "Salutation to Śrī Sūryasāvitrī — I offer this arghya (water)," — thus three times. "For the expiation of the fault of performing this rite at the wrong time, I shall also offer the prāyaścitta (atonement) arghya — this too with the Gāyatrī." "That Sun is Brahman" — saying thus, circumambulate oneself (turn clockwise), touch water. Then perform Ācamana again.

Āsana — Sacred Seat Mantra
आसनं— पृथ्वीत्यस्यमन्त्रस्य, मेरुपृष्ठ ऋषि:, कूर्मो देवता, क्षुतंछंद:, आसने विनियोग: ॥ ओं पृथ्वित्वया धृतालोका देवि त्वं विष्णुनाधृता । त्वञ्च धारय मां देवि पवित्रं कुरुचासनम् ॥ अपसर्पन्तु ते भूता येभूता भूमिसंस्थिता: । येभूता विघ्नकर्तार: ते नश्यंतु शिवाज्ञया ॥ अपक्रामन्तु भूतानि पिशाचा: सर्वतो दिशं । सर्वेषामविरोधेन ब्रह्मकर्मसमारभे ॥
Āsana (Sacred Seat) — For the mantra of Pṛthvī: sage Merupṛṣṭha, deity Kūrma (the Tortoise), meter Kṣutam; its purpose is for the ritual seat. "OM. O Goddess Earth, you hold all the worlds; you yourself are held by Viṣṇu. Hold me, O Goddess, and make my seat pure. May all beings dwelling on the earth move away. May all those who cause obstacles perish by Śiva's command. May all spirits and demons depart in all directions. Without opposition from any, I begin the Brahma-rite."

Gāyatrī Invocation — Summoning of Gāyatrī, Sāvitrī, Sarasvatī and all divine powers
ओं इत्येकाक्षरं ब्रह्म, अग्निर्देवता ब्रह्म इत्याषं, गायत्रं छंदं, परमात्मं स्वरूपं, सायुज्यं विनियोगं, आयातु वरदादेवी अक्षरं ब्रह्मसंमितं । गायत्रीं छंदसां मातेदं ब्रह्मजुषस्वमे ॥ यद्राज्या (दन्हा) कुरुतेपापं तद्राज्या (दन्हा) प्रतिमुच्यते । सर्वर्णें महादेवि संधाविद्ये सरस्वति ॥ ओजोऽसि, सहोऽसि, बलमसि, भ्राजोऽसि, देवानां धामनामासि, विश्वमसि, विश्वायु:, सर्वमसि, सर्वायु:, अभिभूरों, गायत्रीं आवाहयामि, सावित्रीं आवाहयामि, सरस्वतीं आवाहयामि, छन्दर्षीनावाहयामि, श्रियं आवाहयामि, बलं आवाहयामि, गायत्र्या गायत्री छंद:, विश्वामित्र ऋषि:, सविता
"OM — the single-syllable Brahman; deity is Agni; the word is Brahman; the meter is Gāyatrī; the form is the Supreme Self; the purpose is union (sāyujya). May the boon-granting Goddess arrive — Gāyatrī, the syllable equal to Brahman, mother of all meters — may she be pleased with this Brahma-rite of mine. Whatever sin one commits by day (night), one is freed from that by day (night). O great Goddess, O repository of all phonemes, O Sarasvatī who knows all. You are vigour (Ojas), you are might (Sahas), you are strength (Bala), you are radiance (Bhrājas), you are the abode of the gods, you are the universe, you are all life, you are everything, you are all longevity, you are the supreme conquering force — OM. I invoke Gāyatrī, I invoke Sāvitrī, I invoke Sarasvatī, I invoke the Chandas seers, I invoke Śrī (Prosperity), I invoke Bala (Strength). [For the Gāyatrī:] meter is Gāyatrī, sage is Viśvāmitra, deity is Savitā…" [text continues]
